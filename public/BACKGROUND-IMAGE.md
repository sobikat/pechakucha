# Background image (Statue of Liberty / NYC skyline)

**Re-upload your image here for best quality.**  
Place your high-resolution Statue of Liberty (or skyline) image in this `public` folder and name it:

**`nyc-skyline-bg.png`**

Use a high-res file (e.g. 1920×1080 or 2560px wide) so it stays sharp on Retina and large screens. The app will use it automatically. Right now the background is a dark placeholder until you add the file.
